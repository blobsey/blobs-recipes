Chocolate Chip Cookies
=====================

## Wet
2sticks	Unsalted Butter (browned)
1tsp	Table salt
230g	Dark Brown Sugar
180g	Granulated Sugar
2tsp	Vanilla
40ml	Ice (to replace water lost from browning)
75g		Egg (One egg + one egg yolk)

## Dry
386g	All-purpose flour
1tsp	Baking Soda
1tsp	Baking Powder
454g	Chocolate (1 lb)

50g dough balls

Sea salt on top (optional)

350F 11 min


Snickerdoodles
=====================

Cream:
2 Sticks unsalted butter (8oz)
200g granulated white sugar
140g light brown sugar

Combine until lightened in color:
130g egg (2 egg + 2 yolk)
34g Greek Yogurt
10g vanilla

Dry ingredients (Mix in separate bowl):
360g all-purpose flour
2tsp cream of tartar
1/2tsp baking soda
6g (1tsp) salt (if using salted butter, then 4.5g assuming butter is salted at 50mg sodium per tbsp)

Mix the dry and wet until just combined

Chill dough balls for at least ~10 minutes before baking

Cinnamon sugar: 100g granulated white sugar + 2tsp ground cinnamon

50g dough balls 

350F 16 min
